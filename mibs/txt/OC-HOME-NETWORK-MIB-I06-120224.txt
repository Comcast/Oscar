    SYNTAX      MacAddress
    MAX-ACCESS  read-create
    STATUS      current

           provided, the device reports information about the traffic
           for the entire QosSegment identified by QosSegmentId.
           If non-zero value is provided, this device provides 
           information about traffic between its interface, identified
           by the ocHnQosROStatsMacAddress field and the device,

           the device either reports traffic to/from itself or between
           itself and other devices on the network."

    SYNTAX      OCTET STRING

            ':application:', followed by an application name.
